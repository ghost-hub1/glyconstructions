Our Approach

Working Smarter

Our culture is grounded in a fundamental commitment to continuous improvement. No matter how well the last project went, we always look for ways to do more, work smarter. We provide mentoring, training, and education to support your continuous growth.

In turn, we expect you to work collaboratively, think on your feet, be open to learning, adapt to changing conditions, share your skills, talents and experience with others, and never be satisfied with the status quo.

Internships

Are you ready to learn a lot, have some fun, and enjoy the camaraderie with your fellow interns and project teams?

As a GLY intern, you get in-depth exposure to a career in construction with a commercial general contractor that self-performs work. You do “real” work that is directly job related, while also learning about our general operations. Over the course of the summer, you go on jobsite tours, attend events, and meet people from all business units across the company. This rewarding experience is an opportunity to make great industry connections and earn a full-time position at GLY.
