True to Our PNW Roots

We’re proud to be a regional general contractor wholeheartedly committed to our PNW home. We’ve grown considerably since our founders started GLY 50 years ago, but their blueprint is ingrained in our core values today. Take care of our employees, see partners when we look across the table, and work together seamlessly to build a resilient, future-ready community.

Positive Disruption

This region of positive disruption has made us adaptable, eager to pursue and embrace possibility. With an unwavering focus on exceptional customer service, we’re always exploring new ways to transform delivery to achieve greater efficiency, accuracy, and performance.

The heart, soul, and future of GLY is the passion and skill of our employees. We reward performance and encourage continuous learning—cultivating an environment where a diverse group of highly motivated, intellectually curious people thrive. Because we know an unrelenting commitment to inclusion, safety, and teamwork builds a healthier workplace. And a stronger community.

Amy Bergman

Project Executive

With GLY since 1999, Amy has managed projects in all sectors and of all sizes. This versatility is what helps her guide teams in keeping the end goal in mind while addressing day-to-day challenges and complex problem-solving. She is an alumnus of the University of Washington with a BS in Construction Management and Minor in Architecture, has served on the board of her local YMCA, and is active in NAIOP and CREW.
